Privacy and Terms of Service

EurovisionPredictions.xyz is a free service that helps friends and family compete against each other in guessing the outcomes of the annual Eurovision Song Contest.

EurovisionPredictions.xyz believes it is important to clearly explain how we process personal data. In order for you to play and see your and your friends' votes, EurovisionPredictions.xyz processes some of your personal data.

Purposes of processing

DrawNames.com processes your personal data to:

  • Create groups and invite participants.
  • Tailor our website to your wishes and keep track of your personal preferences.
  • Analyze and improve our services.
  • Inform you about our website.

EurovisionPredictions.xyz processes your personal data on the following legal basis:

Processing your personal data is necessary to represent the following justified interest(s) of EurovisionPredictions.xyz: Being able to perform the services for which visitors use EurovisionPredictions.xyz, such as creating groups, picking each country's place, exchanging both of these, and informing users about the winner of their group.

Your privacy

EurovisionPredictions.xyz guarantees your privacy in the following ways:

  • We only process the personal data needed to create groups and invite participants.
  • We do not provide personal data to third parties such as advertisers. Your email address is only used for emails from EurovisionPredictions.xyz.
  • We only place cookies that are necessary for the operation of the website and statistical analysis. These cookies contain anonymous information and stay in your browser for a maximum of one year.
  • We do not place cookies on behalf of third parties (third-party cookies).

We encrypt all pages with SSL / HTTPS: the lock in your browser.

Provision of personal data

If you want to use EurovisionPredictions.xyz you are obliged to enter your name and email address. The following information is not required: gender, age, hobbies & interests and mailing address. If you do not provide this personal data to EurovisionPredictions.xyz, your group will not have as good an idea of who you are.

Secure and safe

EurovisionPredictions.xyz protects and saves your data in the following ways:

  • We handle your personal data carefully. This means, among other things, that we take adequate technical and organizational measures to secure your personal data and protect it against loss or improper processing.
  • Your personal data is stored in a secure environment and is only accessible to authorized DrawNames.com employees insofar as this is necessary for the processing of your personal data as described in this privacy policy.
  • We do not store your personal data longer than necessary for the purposes for which it is processed. When it is no longer necessary to process your personal data, we will anonymize your personal data.

Indemnity

This website has been very carefully developed. However, no rights can be derived from the use of EurovisionPredictions.xyz. EurovisionPredictions.xyz does not accept any liability for the non-functioning or incorrect functioning of the website or the non-arrival or late arrival of messages.

Delete data

The person who has set up a group can remove people (names and email addresses) from the group or delete the whole group.

Your rights

You have the right to submit a request to us to inspect, correct, and/or delete your personal data. You also have the right to ask us to limit the processing of your personal data and you are allowed to object to the processing of your personal data.

As a data subject you also have the right to request EurovisionPredictions.xyz to recover the personal data you have provided to EurovisionPredictions.xyz in a structured, acceptable form which can be read by machines (data portability).
